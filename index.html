Features

Everything a structured runner needs.

No subscriptions required for the core experience. Just download and run.

🏃

Custom Interval Programs

Build warm-ups, hard intervals, rest periods, and cool-downs with repeats. Set goals by distance, time, or both.

📍

GPS Tracking

Kalman-filtered GPS for accurate distance tracking without the freeze-and-jump problem common in other apps.

🔊

Audio Coaching

Spoken cues for segment transitions, pace alerts, and distance milestones — so your eyes stay ahead, not down.

📊

Segment Breakdown

After each run, review per-segment stats: distance, time, pace, and goal performance for every interval.

🗺️

Route Map + Markers

See your GPS route with segment markers pinned exactly where each interval began and ended.

🏆

Personal Records

Automatically tracks your bests for common distances. Every PR gets flagged the moment you cross it.

🌐

Runs Anywhere

Background GPS tracking keeps logging even when your screen is off or you switch apps mid-run.

🆓

Free Run Mode

No program? No problem. Free Run tracks your route, pace, and distance with mile markers — zero setup needed.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pace: Run ("the App") is developed and operated as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Pace: Run:

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and a secure password hash. This is used to identify your account and sync your data across devices.
  • Location data: The App uses your device's GPS to track your running route during active workouts. Location data is recorded only when a run is in progress and you have granted location permissions. We do not collect your location in the background outside of active runs.
  • Run and workout data: We store records of your completed runs including distance, duration, pace, calorie estimates, GPS route coordinates, and segment performance. This data is tied to your account and synced to our secure database.
  • Device information: We may collect basic information about your device type and operating system version for debugging and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality including GPS tracking, run logging, and program storage
  • To sync your data securely across sessions and devices
  • To calculate and display personal records and run history
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account and run data are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider with encryption at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Location Permissions

Pace: Run requests location access ("While Using the App" and background location) to enable GPS run tracking. Background location is used only during an active run session initiated by you. You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will prevent GPS tracking from functioning.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenStreetMap / Flutter Map — map tile rendering (no personal data shared)

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing pacerunningapp@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Pace: Run is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: pacerunningapp@gmail.com
